Each team of 2-3 will be given a robot arm. The goal is to have fun, learn something new, and build something cool. Registration will be capped to our hardware supplies (~ten SO-101 arm pairs currently), 3D printer and lasercutter onsite, compute TBD. See here and here for inspiration.
