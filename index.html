Scope:

Each team of 3-4 will be given a robot arm. The goal is to have fun, learn something new, and build something cool. Registration will be capped to our hardware supplies (6-7 reBots and compute is Jetson Nanos), 3D printer and lasercutter onsite. See here and here for inspiration.

FAQ:

Revolute isn't a company, "Boston Embodied / Physical AI Hackathon" was just a mouthful. We're organizing this because there's something magical about working in-person with other people excited about the same thing. This page is short on details since we don't know them yet -- sign up for final details. Release v1.08 since the plan is for this to be recurring (year/month). Contact email is in the signup form.
